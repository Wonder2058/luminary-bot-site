1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Luminary (the "Bot") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our services on Discord. We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with all applicable data protection laws, including GDPR and CCPA.

2. Information We Collect

  • Server Data: Server IDs, channel IDs, and server settings for configuration and operation.
  • User Data: User IDs, roles, and command usage for moderation, logging, and feature access.
  • Message Data: Content of messages only when required for moderation or logging features, and only as long as necessary.
  • Premium Data: If you purchase premium, we may collect payment-related information as required by our payment processor. We do not store payment details ourselves.
  • Support Data: Information you provide when contacting support or joining our support server.
  • Diagnostic Data: Technical logs and error reports to improve the Bot's reliability and security.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the Bot's features and services.
  • To ensure compliance with Discord's Terms of Service and our own Terms.
  • To respond to support requests and user inquiries.
  • To process premium feature purchases and manage access.
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues, abuse, or security threats.
  • To comply with legal obligations and law enforcement requests.

4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties except as required by law or for the operation of the Bot (e.g., payment processors for premium features).
  • Data may be shared with Discord if required for moderation or abuse reports.
  • We may disclose information if required by law, regulation, or legal process.

5. Data Security

  • We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure, including encryption and access controls.
  • However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.
  • In the event of a data breach, we will notify affected users and relevant authorities as required by law.

6. User Rights

  • You may request deletion of your data by contacting us via the support server.
  • You may request information about what data we hold about you and request corrections if necessary.
  • We will comply with reasonable requests as required by law, including GDPR and CCPA rights.
  • You may opt out of certain data collection features by disabling the Bot or specific features in your server.

7. Data Retention

  • We retain data only as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law.
  • Data may be deleted upon request or when no longer needed for operational purposes.

8. International Data Transfers

  • Your data may be processed and stored in countries outside your own. We take steps to ensure adequate protection for international transfers as required by law.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Continued use of the Bot after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy. It is your responsibility to review this policy regularly.

10. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, join our Support Server.
